The brief

The illustrative brief was to turn an underused urban site into a welcoming market for independent traders, with adaptable stalls, accessible public routes, and practical back-of-house areas.

How the work was approached

The project team coordinated early with stakeholders, created a phased plan to reduce disruption around the site, and kept regular reporting clear for the client and nearby community. A real case study should state the actual project scope, procurement route, project team, and agreed milestones.

What success looked like
